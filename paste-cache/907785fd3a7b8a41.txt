The following Law Constraints are important for our system and have to be adhered to 100%.

1. Distance Between Work-Free Weekends
At minimum every 5 weeks, one work-free weekend (entire Saturday and Sunday) must be allocated

Vacation time replaces work-free weekend allocation if it includes complete weekends

2. Time Balance Limits
Time balance must remain within: +100 / -40 hours during each month

Priority must always be 41 hours a week (with user preference slider)

3. Maximum Work-Time
a person must never exceed 10 hours a day

a person must never exceed 63 hours over 7 work days

a person must never exceed 5 hours without a break (breaks are not relevant right now, might be in the future though)

4. Rest Time Between Shifts
a person must have a minimum of 12 hours of rest time between shifts

5. Night Shift
a person must not exceed 7 concurrent Night Shifts

a person must not exceed 15 Night Shifts in 28 Days

6. Rest Days
a person must have a minimum of 4 Rest-Days (RT) and 2 Compensation Days (CT), so in total a minimum of 6 days of in a month

See 1.): A person must have minimum of 1 weekend each Month as Rest Days (Saturday and Sunday concurrent). Between these Rest Weekends there must not be a gap of more than 5 Weeks
